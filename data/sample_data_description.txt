Dataset: LendingClub Loan Data (2007–2018)

Source:
LendingClub public loan dataset containing loan-level information for consumer loans issued between 2007 and 2018.

Description:
The dataset includes borrower characteristics, loan terms, credit grades, repayment outcomes, and recovery amounts. It is widely used for credit risk modelling research.

Key variables used in this project include:
- loan_amnt (loan amount)
- int_rate (interest rate)
- term (loan duration)
- annual_inc (borrower income)
- dti (debt-to-income ratio)
- fico_range_low / fico_range_high
- grade / sub_grade
- revol_util (revolving utilization)
- recoveries (post-default recoveries)
- loan_status (used to construct default label)

Scale of analysis:
~1.3 million loans were analyzed for modelling.
~518,000 loans were used for portfolio evaluation (2016–2018 test period).

Note:
The original dataset is not included in this repository due to its large size.
Users can download the dataset from the LendingClub public data archive and place it locally if they wish to reproduce the analysis.
